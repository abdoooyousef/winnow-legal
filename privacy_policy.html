Privacy Policy

Winnow — effective April 2026

TL;DR: Winnow scans your media on your device only. Nothing is uploaded without your explicit action. If you sign in and use the Vault, your files are stored encrypted in your personal Firebase account. We never sell your data.

1. Who we are

Winnow ("the app", "we", "us") is developed by Abdelrahman Yousef. Contact: ayn.developer@gmail.com.

2. What Winnow does on your device

Winnow is a storage-cleanup tool. To do its job it needs to read your media library. The following happens entirely on your device and never leaves it unless you explicitly upload something to the Vault:

  • Scanning photos, videos, audio files, and accessible documents
  • Computing hashes (MD5 / perceptual hashes) to detect duplicates
  • Running on-device AI models (TensorFlow Lite) for face detection and blurry-photo detection in future versions
  • Grouping files by folder, size, type, and date
  • Moving deleted items to a local 30-day recycle bin inside the app's private directory

3. Permissions we request

Winnow asks for the following permissions. Each one is clearly explained at the moment it is requested:

  • Read media (photos, videos, audio) — required to scan and analyze your library
  • Manage external storage (optional) — required only if you enable "full cleanup" mode, which lets Winnow remove files outside the standard media folders (such as old downloads)
  • Post notifications (Android 13+) — to tell you when a background scan is complete
  • Internet access — used only if you sign in for the Vault feature

4. The Vault — optional cloud feature

The Vault is an opt-in encrypted cloud storage feature. If you choose to sign in with Google and use the Vault:

  • Your Google account email, display name, and profile photo are stored to identify your account
  • Files you explicitly upload are stored in Firebase Storage under a path tied to your user ID
  • File metadata (name, size, folder) is stored in Firestore
  • Files in the Cards folder are end-to-end encrypted with a PIN you set; only you can decrypt them

5. Data we do NOT collect

  • We do not collect your location
  • We do not read your contacts, messages, or call logs
  • We do not use third-party analytics or advertising SDKs
  • We do not sell or share your data with third parties
  • We do not upload any media without your explicit action

6. Your rights

You may at any time:

  • Delete any file from the Vault (it is permanently removed from our servers)
  • Delete your account by going to Settings → Account → Delete account (this removes all Vault data permanently)
  • Sign out to stop all cloud syncing
  • Uninstall the app to remove all local data
  • Contact us to request a full data export or deletion: ayn.developer@gmail.com

7. Third-party services we use

  • Firebase (Google LLC) — authentication, file storage, metadata. Firebase Privacy Policy
  • Google Sign-In — to identify Vault users. Google Privacy Policy
  • Google Play Billing — used if you subscribe to a paid plan. We never see your payment details

8. Data retention

  • Local recycle bin: 30 days, then permanently deleted on-device
  • Vault files: retained until you delete them or cancel your subscription (with a 30-day grace period)
  • Account record: deleted within 30 days of account deletion request

9. Children

Winnow is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

10. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy. Material changes will be announced via in-app notification. Continued use after an update means you accept the revised policy.
